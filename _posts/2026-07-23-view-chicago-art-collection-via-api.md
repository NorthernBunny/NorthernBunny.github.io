I built an interactive web gallery that connects directly to the [Art Institute of Chicago API](https://api.artic.edu/docs/). It pulls random public-domain artworks across various mediums—ranging from oil paintings, sculptures, and watercolors to photography, textiles, and Japanese woodblock prints.

### Interactive Controls

* **Tap/Click Right:** Skip to the next artwork
* **Tap/Click Left:** Go back to the previous artwork
* **Tap/Click Center:** Pause or resume the 10-second slideshow timer
