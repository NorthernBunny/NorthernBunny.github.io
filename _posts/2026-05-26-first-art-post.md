Decided to start a personal site. Figured I would learn some stuff by doing it, which I already have! It is to be a place to document personal projects I am working on, to refer to later and to share with others. So with this first art post, I will say that I have always liked creativity, and I think art is a way to stimulate the mind and senses. I am currently signed up to a continuing education class for Acryllic painting. I have not painted before. It is quite challenging for sure. You have to see things with respect to lights and darks, forms, colors, and the technical skills to execute. So need to be able to judge lights and darks in a picture for example you want to paint, which is not so easy...for example the darkest dark in a yellow will not be as dark as a dark in something that is blue, but it might look like its the darkest. Mixing colors to match, using the different brushes, mediums to change the nature of the acryllic quick drying paint, correct amount of water, layering, etc. Anyways, here are my current 5 paintings that I have produced :)
