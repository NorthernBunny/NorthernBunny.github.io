Decided to start a personal site. Figured I would learn some stuff by doing it—which I already have! This site is meant to be a place to document the personal projects I am working on, both to refer to later and to share with others. 

With this first art post, I have always appreciated creativity and believe that art is a fantastic way to stimulate the mind and senses. I am currently signed up for a continuing education class for acrylic painting. Having never painted before, I can say it is quite challenging for sure. It forces you to completely re-evaluate how you see the world with respect to lights, darks, forms, and colors, all while developing the technical skills to execute them on canvas. 

For example, learning to accurately judge lights and darks (values) in a picture you want to paint is incredibly difficult. The darkest value in a bright yellow will never be as deep as a dark value in a blue, yet contextually it might look like the darkest spot in that area. Beyond values, there is a massive learning curve to mixing colors to accurately match what you see, selecting the right brushes, using mediums to alter the working time of quick-drying acrylic paint, managing the correct amount of water, and understanding how to layer properly.
